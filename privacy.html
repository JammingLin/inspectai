Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

ForensIQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Who We Are

ForensIQ ("we", "us", "our") operates the website inspectai.cc and provides AI-powered home inspection report generation services. For privacy inquiries, contact us at: privacy@inspectai.cc

2. Data We Collect

Waitlist registration: When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address solely to notify you when ForensIQ launches.

Usage data: We may collect anonymized analytics data (page views, browser type) to improve our service. No personally identifiable information is included.

Home Inspection files (future product): Files uploaded for report generation are processed in isolated environments and permanently deleted immediately after processing. We do not store, analyze, or share uploaded home inspection data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Email address: to send a one-time launch notification
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling

4. Data Retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until ForensIQ launches or until you request deletion. Uploaded home inspection files are deleted immediately after processing and are never retained.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Union or California, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data at any time
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@inspectai.cc. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the website to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Home Inspection files are processed in isolated, ephemeral environments with no persistent storage. We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Their privacy policy applies to infrastructure-level data handling. We do not integrate third-party advertising or tracking services.

9. Children's Privacy

ForensIQ is intended for professional use only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify waitlist members of significant changes via email. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests: privacy@inspectai.cc
